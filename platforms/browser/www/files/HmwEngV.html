God made the sun, moon, stars and the earth.Vajtswv tsim lub hnub, lub hli, cov hnubqub thiab lub ntiajteb.
God made the animals, the trees, mountains, fish, birds and all the living creatures.Vajtswv tsim tsiajtxhu, tej xyoob ntoo, tej roob, ntses, nasnoog thiab txhua yam tsiaj uas muaj sia.
God made people, loved them, and gave them everything they needed to have a happy life.Vajtswv tsim tibneeg, hlub tibneeg, thiab muab txhua yam lawv yuav tsum muaj ua rau lawv tau lub neej zoo siab.
People disobeyed God and did what they wanted to do instead of what God wanted them to do.Tibneeg tsis mloog Vajtswv lus thiab tau mus ua raws li lawv lub siab nyiam ua xwb, tsis ua raws li Vajtswv hais kom lawv ua.
After people went away from God, they forgot God and began to worship things like stones, trees and evil spirits.Tom qab tibneeg khiav ntawm Vajtswv mus lawm, lawv tsis nco qab Vajtswv thiab pib hawm lwm yam xws li tej pobzeb, tej xyoob ntoo thiab cov ntsujplig phem.
Also, people had to work very hard to earn theirliving.Thiab, tibneeg yuav tau ua dejnum hnyav heev thiaj tau noj tau haus.
But God still loved people and sent His messengers to tell them the good news of His love and forgiveness.Tiamsis Vajtswv tseem hlub tibneeg thiab tau txib Nws cov tubtxib mus qhia rau lawv txog txoj xov zoo hais txog Nws txojkev hlub thiab kev zam txim.
Do you know the four things God wants you to know?Koj puas paub txog plaub yam uas Vajtswv xav kom koj paub?
1

God LOVES you and offers a wonderful PLAN for your life.

 1

Vajtswv HLUB koj thiab xav muab ib lub TSWVYIM zoo kawg nkaus rau koj lub neej.
God's Love VAJTSWV TXOJKEV HLUB
"God loved the world so much that He gave His only Son (Jesus Christ) that everyone who believes in Him should not die but have eternal life." John 3:16 “Vajtswv hlub ntiajteb kawg li Nws muab Nws tibleeg Tub (Yexus Khetos) kom txhua tus uas ntseeg Nws yuav tsis tuag tiamsis tau txojsia ntev mus ibtxhis.” Yauhas 3:16
God's Plan VAJTSWV LUB TSWVYIM
Jesus said, ‘I came that you can have life, life in all its fullness’ (the best kind of life). John 10:10 Yexus haistias, “Kuv los kom nej muaj txojsia, lub neej puvnpo (lub neej uas zoo tshajplaws).” Yauhas 10:10
Why is it that most people are not living this full, best life? Yog vim li cas coob tus tibneeg tsis muaj lub neej puvnpo no?
Because... Rau qhov…
2

People are sinners. We do things that are wrong and this keeps us a long way from God. We cannot know and enjoy God's love and plan for our life.

 2

Txhua tus yog tibneeg txhaum. Peb ua tej yam uas tsis yog thiab tej no ua rau peb txav deb ntawm Vajtswv. Peb paub tsistau thiab zoo siab tsis tau rau Vajtswv txojkev hlub thiab lub tswvyim rau peb lub neej.
People are Sinful TIBNEEG MUAJ KEV TXHAUM
‘All people have sinned.’ (They are not living the way God wants them to live.) Romans 3:23 “Txhua tus tibneeg tau ua txhaum.’ (Lawv tsis ua neej li uas Vajtswv hais kom lawv ua). Loos 3:23
People were created to be friends of God, but because we wanted to please ourselves, we chose to go our own way without God. Our friendship with God was spoiled. This going our own way means doing things that God does not want us to do or taking no interest in God. This is what the Bible calls sin. Vajtswv tsim tibneeg nrog Vajtswv ua phoojywg, tiamsis vim peb tsuas xav ua kom zoo peb lub siab xwb, peb tau xaiv taug peb txojkev tsis muaj Vajtswv. Peb txojkev nrog Vajtswv ua phoojywg tau puastsuaj lawm. Qhov peb taug peb txojkev no yog peb ua tej uas Vajtswv tsis xav kom peb ua losyog tsis quavntsej txog Vajtswv. Vajlugkub hu qhov no haistias yog kev txhaum.
PEOPLE ARE KEPT A LONG WAY FROM GOD. TIBNEEG RAUG CAIS DEB NTAWM VAJTSWV.
"The payment for sin is death." Romans 6:23 “Tus nqi them kev txhaum yog kev tuag.” Loos 6:23
This death is more than just our body dying – it means spiritual death – being kept a long way from God and not having any friendship with Him. Txojkev tuag no yog tshaj li uas peb lub cev tuag xwb – nws yog tuag sab ntsujplig thiab – uas yog raug cais deb ntawm Vajtswv thiab ua tsis tau phoojywg nrog Vajtswv.
God is holy and good while people are sinful. The difference between God and people is so great it is like a great, deep valley keeping them a long way from each other. People are always trying to reach God and find the full, best life by doing things themselves, such as trying to be good, obeying the laws of the land and being kind to others. But people can never reach God by their own effort. Vajtswv yog tus dawbhuv thiab zoo tiamsis tibneeg yog cov muaj kev txhaum. Qhov txawv ntawm Vajtswv thiab tibneeg loj heev ib yam li ib lub kwjha loj thiab tob uas cais ob leeg lawm. Tiabneeg tabmeeg xav mus cuag Vajtswv kom tau lub neej npuvnpo, lub neej zoo lawv tus kheej ua, xws li ua tibneeg zoo, coj raws li kevcai ntiajteb thiab ua zoo rau lwm tus. Tiamsis tibneeg yeej mus cuag tsis tau Vajtswv dhau los ntawm lawv tus dag zog.
3

Jesus Christ is the ONLY way God has given for people to get rid of their sins. Through him you can know and enjoy God's love and plan for your life.

 3

Yexus Khetos tsuas yog TIB txojkev uas Vajtswv muab rau tibneeg tshem tawm lawv tej kev txhaum. Dhau los ntawm Nws, koj paub tau thiab zoo siab tau rau Vajtswv txojkev hlub thiab lub tswvyim rau koj lub neej.
HE DIED IN OUR PLACE NWS TUAG NYOB PEB LUB CHAW
"But God has shown how much He loves us; it was while we were still sinners that Jesus Christ died for us.” Romans 5:8 “Tiamsis Vajtswv tau qhia tias Nws hlub peb npaum li cas; nws yog thaum peb tseem yog tibneeg txhaum Yexus Khetos tau tuag rau peb.” Loos 5:8
On the cross He took the punishment for our sins. Saum tus ntoo khaublig, Nws tau raug txim rau peb cov kev txhaum.
HE ROSE FROM THE DEAD NWS TAU SAWV HAUV QHOV TUAG ROV LOS
“Jesus Christ died for our sins… He was buried……He was raised to life on the third day just as He told His followers he would be … “Yexus Khetos tuag rau peb cov kev txhaum…Nws tau muab faus.. Hnub peb Nws tau sawv rov los muaj sia nyob ib yam li Nws tau qhia rau Nws cov thwjtim haistias Nws yuav ua li ntawd…
…He appeared to Peter and then to all the twelve disciples. Then He appeared to more than five hundred of His followers at one time." I Corinthians 15:3-6 “…Nws tshwm rau Pestus thiab tom qab rau tagnrho kaum ob tug thwjtim. Ces Nws tshwm rau ntau tshaj li uas tsib puas leej cov uas raws Nws qab ua ib zaug.” 1 Kauleethaus 15:3-6
He Is the Only Way to God NWS YOG TIB TXOJKEV XWB
Jesus Christ said: "I am the Way, I am the Truth, I am the Life. No man goes to God except by me." John 14:6 Yexus Khetos haistias, “Kuv yog Txojkev, Kuv yog Qhovtseeb, Kuv yog Txojsia. Yeej tsis muaj leejtwg mus cuag tau Leejtxiv tsuas yog los ntawm Kuv xwb.” Yauhas 14:6
God put a bridge over the great deep valley that keeps us away from Him when He sent His Son, Jesus Christ, to die on the cross in our place. Vajtswv tau tuam ib tus choj rau lub kwjha loj thiab tob uas cais peb tawm ntawm Vajtswv thaum Nws txib Nws tus Tub, Yexus Khetos, los tuag rau saum tus ntoo khaublig nyob peb lub chaw.
It is not enough to know these three things ... Paub peb yam li no xwb tseem tsis tau zoo txaus…
4

We need to BELIEVE in Jesus Christ as our own personal Saviour and Lord. Then we can know and enjoy God's love and plan for our lives.

 4

Peb yuav tau NTSEEG Yexus Khetos ua peb tus Cawmseej thiab tus Tswv. Ces peb thiaj paub tau thiab muaj tau kev zoo siab rau Vajtswv txojkev hlub thiab lub tswvyim rau peb lub neej.
WE NEED TO BELIEVE IN JESUS CHRIST PEB YUAV TAU NTSEEG YEXUS KHETOS
"Some however did accept Him; so He gave them the right to become God’s children." John 1:12 “Tiamsis muaj tej txhia txais Nws; ces Nws muab cai rau lawv ua Vajtswv cov menyuas.” Yauhas 1:12
GOD’S GRACE SAVES US THROUGH FAITH VAJTSWV TXOJ KOOBHMOOV CAWM PEB DHAU LOS NTAWM KEV NTSEEG
"It is by God’s grace that you have been saved, through faith (that trusts Him). It is not through your own effort. It is God’s gift." Ephesians 2:8 “Nws yog Vajtswv txoj koobhmoov nej thiaj tau dim, dhau los ntawm kev ntseeg. Nws tsis yog dhau los ntawm nej tus dag zog. Nws yog Vajtswv yam khoom pub.” Efexaus 2:8
WE SHOW OUR BELIEF IN JESUS CHRIST BY ACCEPTING HIS INVITATION PEB NTSEEG YEXUS KHETOS LOS NTAWM KEV TXAIS NWS LO LUS CAW
Jesus Christ said, “Listen, I stand at the door (of your heart) and knock; if anyone hears my voice and opens the door, I will come in to him (your heart).” Revelation 3:20 Yexus Khetos haistias, “Mloog, Kuv sawv ntawm qhovrooj (koj lub siab) thiab khob; yog leejtwg hnov Kuv lub suab hu thiab qhib lub qhovrooj, Kuv yuav los rau hauv nws (lub siab).” Tshwmsim 3:20
To believe in Jesus Christ means to follow God’s way instead of our own way, to trust Christ to come into our lives, to forgive our sins and make us what He wants us to be. Ntseeg Yexus Khetos yog taug Vajtswv txojkev tsis yog taug yus txojkev, peb yuav tau ntseeg Yexus rau peb lub neej, ua tus zam peb tej kev txhaum thiab cia Nws hloov peb li uas Nws pom zoo.
It is not enough to agree in our minds that what God wants is true or to have special feelings of being happy or sad when we think about these things. Nws tseem zoo tsis tau txaus qhov uas peb paub haistias tej uas Vajtswv xav kom peb ua yog qhov tseeb es ua rau peb muaj kev zoo siab losyog tu siab thaum uas peb xav txog tej no…

WHAT JESUS HAS DONE FOR YOUTEJ YEXUS TAU UA RAU KOJ
When you believe in Jesus as your Saviour and Lord you can be sure that:Thaum koj ntseeg Yexus ua koj tus Cawmseej thiab tus Tswv, koj yeej paub tseeb haistias:
  • Jesus came into your life. (Revelations 3:30, Colossians 1:27)
  • Khetos los rau hauv koj lub neej (Tshwmsim 3:30; Khaulauxais 1:27)
  • Your sins were forgiven (Colossians 1:14)
  • Vajtswv tau zam koj tej kev txhaum lawm (Khaulauxais 1:14)
  • You became a child of God (John 1:12)
  • Koj yog Vajtswv ib tug menyuam (Yauhas 1:12)
  • You have eternal life. (John 5:24)
  • Koj tau txojsia ntev (Yauhas 5:24)
  • You began the new life for which God made you. (John 10:10)
  • Koj pib ua lub neej tshiab uas Vajtswv coj koj mus ua (Yauhas 10:10)
